Learn Ruby on Rails

Last updated 2 August 2013

New to Rails? See What is Ruby on Rails? for an introduction.

Community website featuring recommended resources for learning Ruby on Rails. Anyone can suggest resources to learn Rails and add links. All contributions are screened by the site curators to prevent spam.

You may wish to learn Ruby (the programming language), learn Rails (the web application development platform), or find resources for learning in your local area.

Online

Developers in the Rails community often debate whether you should learn Ruby before you attempt to learn Rails. To use Rails, you’ll need to become proficient with Ruby. But you have a choice. You can study Ruby first, or you can become familiar with Ruby while learning Rails. Either way, these resources will help you learn Ruby.

Learn Ruby

Newcomers are blessed with many ways to learn Rails. You’ll find books, videos, classes, online tutorials — and more — to suit all learning styles and skill levels.

Locally

The best learning comes from collaboration with students and mentors. You’ll find many opportunities to join instructor-led classes, meet study partners, or join study groups in technology hubs such as San Francisco, New York, and London. Even in smaller metro areas, you can find mentors, study partners, and workshops. This site doesn’t yet offer comprehensive listings but if you have resources to recommend in your area, please make your suggestion.
